Message from Head of Office

It gives me immense satisfaction to present the Annual Report of the MSME Development and Facilitation Office (DFO), Jammu for the financial year 2025–26. This report reflects our continuous efforts to foster a dynamic MSME ecosystem, support entrepreneurship, and promote inclusive industrial development across Jammu & Kashmir & Ladakh UTs. In line with the national vision of Atmanirbhar Bharat, our office has played an active role in implementing key flagship schemes of the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises. These include PM Vishwakarma, Procurement and Marketing Support (PMS), Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), MSE–Cluster Development Programme (MSE–CDP), ZED Certification Scheme, MSME-LEAN, Innovation and IC Schemes, and the CHAMPIONS platform for real-time support and grievance redressal. Through a series of outreach initiatives—including capacity-building programs, entrepreneur awareness sessions, vendor development meets, and direct handholding support—we have strived to bridge information gaps and bring the benefits of these schemes to the grassroots. Special emphasis was laid on integrating artisans, rural entrepreneurs, and women-led enterprises into the formal economy, ensuring that no segment is left behind. This report not only documents the progress we’ve made but also highlights our on-ground collaborations, stakeholder engagement, and success stories that illustrate the impact of our work. It stands as a testament to our collective commitment to enabling self-reliance, innovation, and economic resilience within the MSME sector. I would like to express my sincere appreciation to the entire team at MSME DFO Jammu for their professionalism, dedication, and teamwork throughout the year. Their contribution has been pivotal in executing the Ministry’s mission effectively. As we move forward, let this report serve as both a reflection of our achievements and a reminder of the responsibility we carry in driving sustainable growth for the entrepreneurs and enterprises of Jammu & Kashmir & Ladakh UTs.


Amit Kumar Tamaria, IEDS

Jammu & Kashmir – At A Glance

Jammu and Kashmir, a Union Territory of India since October 31, 2019, holds a unique position in the country's administrative and cultural framework. Located in the northernmost part of India, it is bordered by the Union Territory of Ladakh to the east and shares international boundaries with Pakistan and China.

The region is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, diverse geography, and rich cultural heritage, encompassing the scenic Kashmir Valley, the majestic Himalayan ranges, and the historical region of Jammu.

Administratively, the Union Territory is composed of two divisions – Jammu and Kashmir – with a total of 20 districts. The UT functions under the administrative framework laid down by the Jammu and Kashmir Reorganization Act, 2019. The Lieutenant Governor serves as the constitutional head of the territory while various departments implement developmental initiatives and governance reforms.

Jammu and Kashmir continues to progress on the path of development with a focus on infrastructure, education, healthcare, tourism, entrepreneurship, and digital governance. Significant efforts are being undertaken to strengthen grassroots democracy, improve service delivery, and foster inclusive growth across both urban and rural regions.

This Annual Report highlights the initiatives, achievements, and future plans of MSME Development & Facilitation Office, Jammu, aligned with the broader vision of sustainable development, self-reliance, and citizen-centric governance in the Union Territory of Jammu & Kashmir.

MSME Development & Facilitation Office, Jammu

Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises, Government of India

The MSME-Development and Facilitation Office (DFO), Jammu, situated at Industrial Estate, Jammu, functions under the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises, Government of India. The office is entrusted with the promotion and development of the MSME sector across all 20 districts of Jammu & Kashmir and 02 districts of Ladakh UT.

The DFO provides comprehensive techno-managerial consultancy, advisory services, and facilitation support to both existing enterprises and aspiring entrepreneurs, including traditional artisans and micro-enterprises.

To foster entrepreneurial spirit among educated youth and aspiring business owners, the office regularly organizes Entrepreneurship Awareness Programmes (EAPs), Entrepreneurship Skill Development Programmes (ESDPs), Management Development Programmes (MDPs), Vendor Development Programmes (VDPs), and other capacity-building initiatives tailored to local needs.

The DFO also plays a critical role in enhancing awareness among MSMEs regarding technological advancements, quality standards, digital transformation, market access, and government support schemes, thereby promoting competitiveness and sustainable growth in the MSME ecosystem.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To create a vibrant, self-reliant, and inclusive MSME ecosystem that nurtures innovation, entrepreneurship, and sustainable industrial development across the region. We envision empowering aspiring entrepreneurs, artisans, and existing enterprises through access to technology, skill development, market opportunities, and institutional support. By fostering a culture of enterprise, competitiveness, and continuous innovation, we aim to generate employment, strengthen local economies, and enhance the contribution of MSMEs to socio-economic growth. Through effective implementation of government initiatives and stakeholder collaboration, we strive to realize the vision of Atmanirbhar Bharat and build a resilient, globally competitive MSME sector that drives inclusive and sustainable development.

Mission

  • To facilitate the growth and development of Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises Including Traditional Artisan through effective implementation of MSME schemes and policies.
  • To strengthen state-level support for national initiatives like Startup India, Stand- Up India, and Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan.
  • To promote entrepreneurship culture across rural and urban areas of the state.
  • To act as a bridge between entrepreneurs, financial institutions, and government departments.
  • To ensure last-mile delivery of support services including credit linkage, market access, technology upgradation, and capacity building.

Entrepreneurship Awareness Programmes (EAPs)

Implementation of RAMP Project in Jammu & Kashmir

Overview

The Ministry of MSME, Government of India, has approved the Strategic Investment Plan (SIP) under the Raising and Accelerating MSME Performance (RAMP) Programme with support from the World Bank. The programme is being actively implemented across Jammu & Kashmir to strengthen the MSME ecosystem and enhance enterprise competitiveness.

Objectives and Scope

  • Strengthening MSME implementation capacity across States and UTs.
  • Improving convergence and effectiveness of MSME schemes.
  • Promoting innovation, entrepreneurship and competitiveness.
  • Enhancing quality standards and market access.
  • Facilitating adoption of Industry 4.0 technologies.
  • Supporting green transition and financial inclusion.
  • Improving centre-state coordination and delayed payment resolution.

Financial Details

  • Total Outlay: ₹6,062.45 Crore
  • Funding: Government of India & World Bank
  • Duration: 2021-22 to 2025-26
  • Coverage: 5.55 Lakh MSMEs across India

New Sub-Schemes under RAMP

1. MSE GIFT Scheme

  • Supports adoption of green technologies.
  • Provides interest subvention assistance.
  • Offers credit guarantee support.

2. MSE SPICE Scheme

  • Promotes circular economy projects.
  • Supports recycling and sustainable production.
  • Provides credit-linked subsidies.
  • Contributes towards India's Net Zero 2070 goals.

3. MSE ODR Scheme

  • Online dispute resolution mechanism for delayed payments.
  • AI-enabled technology platform.
  • Fast and efficient grievance resolution system.
  • Implemented in collaboration with NICSI.

Implementation Partners

Partner Agency Scheme
SIDBI MSE GIFT & MSE SPICE
NICSI MSE ODR

Expected Impact

The RAMP Programme is expected to improve MSME productivity, competitiveness, sustainability, financial access, innovation, and market integration while strengthening the overall MSME ecosystem in Jammu & Kashmir.
